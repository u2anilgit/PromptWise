Use the PromptWise `insights_report` tool to surface ranked recommendations across
routing (downgrade/escalate), cost (top drivers & spend anomalies), quality (declining
met-rate & eval regressions), and budget (projected overrun) — computed offline over the
local outcome, cost, and eval telemetry. Summarize the top items with their estimated
impact and confidence, and call out any that would save spend or fix a regression.
